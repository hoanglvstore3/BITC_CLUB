ABOUT US

BITC - BTEC INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CLUB

BITC Information Technology Club was born on April 26, 2022. It is a pioneering Information Technology club founded by students who love technology at British College BTEC FPT Da Nang. This is considered a development place for young people with the same passion in the field of software technology. With enthusiasm from the management team, it is promised that this will become a place to develop professional capacity for members.
